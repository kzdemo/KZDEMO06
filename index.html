What We Do

Practice Areas

Complex matters across five focused disciplines, handled by experienced litigators who know how to win.

Business Litigation

We represent companies and executives in high-stakes commercial disputes including breach of contract, business torts, partnership disputes, and complex fraud claims. Our litigators have tried cases in state and federal courts across the country.

Employment

From wage-and-hour class actions to executive compensation disputes and wrongful termination claims, we counsel employers and senior executives through California's complex employment landscape and beyond.

Intellectual Property

We handle trade secret misappropriation, patent disputes, trademark and copyright infringement, and technology licensing conflicts. We protect the innovations that matter most to your business.

Internal Investigations

When boards and audit committees need independent counsel, we conduct thorough, privileged investigations into financial irregularities, misconduct, and compliance failures — and advise on remediation and disclosure.

Shareholder Disputes

Derivative actions, merger litigation, books-and-records demands, and minority shareholder disputes. We represent investors, boards, and special committees in matters that determine the future of companies.
