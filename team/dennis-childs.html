Dennis Childs represents a wide range of industries with their employment-law needs.

A former partner at the international law firm Cooley LLP, Dennis brings decades of experience counseling and defending employers in complex workplace disputes — from single-plaintiff discrimination and wrongful termination suits to wage-and-hour class actions and executive departures.

Dennis is regularly named among the best in his field by various publications, including Southern California Super Lawyers (Labor & Employment), the San Diego Daily Transcript's "Top San Diego County Attorneys" (Employment & Labor), and San Diego Magazine (Top Lawyers).

His approach is pragmatic: he understands that employment disputes are rarely just about the law — they involve people, culture, and the long-term health of a business. Dennis counsels clients through these complexities with judgment, discretion, and business sense.
