Shaun Paisley represents a wide range of industries in high-stakes matters, including contract disputes, employment class actions, and fraud cases.

A former partner at the leading international law firm Kirkland & Ellis LLP, Shaun has extensive appellate experience, having briefed and won appeals before the U.S. Supreme Court, several federal Courts of Appeals, the California Supreme Court, and the California Court of Appeal.

Most notably, in DIRECTV, Inc. v. Imburgia, 136 S. Ct. 463 (2015), Shaun successfully represented DIRECTV in its appeal to the U.S. Supreme Court concerning the enforceability of arbitration agreements.

Shaun founded his own litigation practice, Paisley Law, after relocating in 2017. He now heads up Koning Zollar's first East Coast location in New Haven, where he leads the firm's trial and appellate advocacy on complex commercial matters.
