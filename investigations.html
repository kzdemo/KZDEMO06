We effectively and discretely investigate all types of professional impropriety.

Our attorneys have extensive experience in conducting internal investigations for public and private corporations, as well as for audit committees and other special committees. Types of investigations include investigations arising from allegations of accounting fraud, workplace discrimination and sexual harassment, government contracting fraud, insider trading, and executive improprieties.

We understand the importance of conducting investigations that are focused and thorough, yet discreet, and that enable management to readily understand what, if anything, has happened and how to respond.
